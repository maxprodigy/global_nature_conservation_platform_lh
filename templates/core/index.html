    WILDLIFE CONSERVATION

    Despite a rise in conservation efforts in Africa to 17% in 2024 compared to 15.55% in 2020, the effectiveness of the measures put in place to conserve nature has been hit with challenges such as inadequate funding, human recklessness, habitat encroachment, and climate change.

    OCEAN PRESERVATION

    Africa's vast ocean resources are cruicial for the continent's economic development, food security, and environmental health. However, these riches face significant threats that jeopardize their long term sustainability.

    HABITAT PROTECTION

    Across Africa, a wave of habitat destruction is jeopardizing the continent's remarkable biodiversity and undermining it's ecological health. This widespread loss threatens not just wildlife, but also the wellbeing of human communities.
